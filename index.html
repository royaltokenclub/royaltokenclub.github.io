How to be part of royal family?

Why be part of royal family?

Which royal categories exist?

There are 4 categories: Gold, Silver, Black and Diamond.

What are the difference between categories?

The members of each category will be included in groups (Telgram/Discord according to their royalty category) and will have specific benefits, draw and NFT depending on that category.
